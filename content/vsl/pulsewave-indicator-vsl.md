## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Voice:** Calm, measured, authoritative. NOT hype-y. NOT "guru energy." Think documentary narrator meets experienced trader. Someone who's seen everything and isn't trying to impress you.

**Pacing:** Slow enough to build tension. Every pause is intentional. Let the chart reactions speak for themselves — don't narrate over them. When price bounces off a level, shut up and let the viewer see it happen.

**Screen recording:** Use dark TradingView theme. PulseWave's green/red zones should be the most colorful thing on screen. No other indicators loaded. Clean, minimal, professional.

**Music:** Low ambient synth. Think Hans Zimmer at 10% volume. Builds slightly during the proof section. Drops out completely during the CTA. Never competes with the voice.

**Text overlays:** Minimal. White on dark background. Only for the hook and the CTA. Let the chart do the talking everywhere else.

**Length target:** 8-9 minutes. This is long enough to build conviction, short enough to hold attention. If someone watches past minute 2, they're watching the whole thing.
