## 5. Free Trial Strategy: 14-Day Premium Trial

**Why 14 Days:**
- Long enough to see trading results (2 weeks = multiple trading opportunities)
- Matches typical trading psychology learning curve
- Allows completion of full onboarding sequence
- Creates habit formation (studies show 14 days for habit creation)

**Trial Structure:**
- **Credit card required:** Reduces low-quality signups by 60%
- **Full Pulse tier access:** No feature limitations build trust
- **Personal onboarding:** Welcome email from Mason personally
- **Usage-based messaging:** Different flows for active vs. inactive users

**Trial Conversion Tactics:**
- **Day 3:** "How to make your first profitable trade" tutorial
- **Day 7:** Success stories from similar traders
- **Day 10:** Early upgrade incentive (2 months free)
- **Day 12:** Personal email from customer success with offer
- **Day 14:** Exit survey with potential win-back offer

**Alternative: Freemium Tier Consideration**
- Limited to basic charts and news
- No AI assistant, limited signals
- Heavy upgrade prompts
- **Pros:** Lower barrier to entry, larger user base
- **Cons:** Higher support costs, lower conversion rates
- **Verdict:** Start with 14-day trial, consider freemium in Month 6 if needed

## 8. Pricing Psychology ($97-197 Strategy)

### Why These Price Points Work:

**$97 (Pulse) - The "Just Below $100" Psychological Barrier**
- Feels like "double digits" not "triple digits"
- Classic charm pricing (reduces perceived cost by 30%)
- Accessible for beginner traders
- Higher conversion rate, lower churn

**$147 (Wave) - The "Sweet Spot" Premium**
- 50% increase feels justified for premium features
- Most popular tier (price anchoring effect)
- Higher LTV while maintaining reasonable churn
- Appeals to intermediate traders ready to invest more

**$197 (Tsunami) - The "Professional" Tier**
- Just under $200 psychological barrier
- Appeals to serious/advanced traders
- Lower volume but higher lifetime value
- Creates aspirational upgrade path

### Annual Discount Strategy:

**Standard Offer:** 2 months free (16.7% discount)
- $97/mo becomes $970 annually (vs $1,164)
- $147/mo becomes $1,470 annually (vs $1,764)  
- $197/mo becomes $1,970 annually (vs $2,364)

**Psychological Benefits:**
- Improves cash flow and reduces churn
- "2 months free" sounds better than "17% off"
- Annual commitment increases customer lifetime value
- Creates budget planning opportunity for traders

### Black Friday/Seasonal Pricing:

**Black Friday Mega Deal (Annual Only):**
- Pulse: $67/mo when paid annually ($804 vs $1,164) - 31% off
- Wave: $97/mo when paid annually ($1,164 vs $1,764) - 34% off
- Tsunami: $147/mo when paid annually ($1,764 vs $2,364) - 25% off

**Q1 "New Year, New Trader" Promotion:**
- 40% off first 3 months for new customers
- Creates urgency and capitalizes on resolution-making psychology

**Summer "Trading Camp" Bundle:**
- 6-month commitment with 20% discount
- Includes bonus coaching calls and exclusive content
- Appeals to serious traders preparing for fall markets
